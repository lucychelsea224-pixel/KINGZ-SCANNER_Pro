Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

KingzScan Pro ("our Service") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our document scanning and processing web application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Document Content

When you use KingzScan Pro to scan or upload documents:

  • Processing: Your documents are processed locally on your device in your browser
  • No Server Storage: By default, your documents are NOT sent to our servers or third-party services
  • Temporary Data: Document data may exist temporarily in your browser's memory during the editing session
  • Exports: Any exported files (PDF, PNG, ZIP) are completely under your control

2.2 Usage Data

We may collect certain information automatically about your browsing activity:

  • Device type, operating system, and browser information
  • IP address and general location data
  • Page views, clicks, and interaction patterns
  • Referrer sources and time spent on the application
Note: This information is collected through Google Analytics and Google AdSense for analytics and advertising purposes only. No personal document content is tracked.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the KingzScan Pro service
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize user experience
  • To debug technical issues and ensure service reliability
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage & Security

Local Processing: KingzScan Pro is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that performs all document processing locally in your browser. This means:

  • Your documents never leave your device unless you explicitly share or export them
  • We do not store your documents on our servers
  • You have full control over your data
  • The application works offline after the initial load

Offline Functionality: The service uses a service worker to cache essential files, allowing the application to function without an internet connection while maintaining privacy.

5. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with third-party services for analytics and advertising:

  • Google Analytics: Used to track anonymized usage statistics and improve our service
  • Google AdSense: Used to display contextual advertisements. Google may use cookies and other tracking technologies

Please refer to Google's privacy policies for more information about how they handle your data:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google's Use of Cookies

6. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies for:

  • Session management and preference storage
  • Analytics tracking (Google Analytics)
  • Advertising functionality (Google AdSense)

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. However, disabling cookies may affect certain functionality of the application.

7. Children's Privacy

KingzScan Pro is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

8. Data Retention

  • Document Data: Stored only in your browser session; cleared when you close the application or clear browser data
  • Analytics Data: Retained by Google Analytics according to their retention policies
  • Cookies: Set expiration based on their purpose; most session cookies clear when you close your browser
